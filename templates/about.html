What is smajik?
Smajik finds the most positive and negative recent tweets about a topic

How does smajik work?

Smajik sources recent tweets that have the queried keyword using Tweepy's API. Then Smajik runs the tweets through Python's TextBlob library for sentiment analysis and sifts through the top positive and negative tweets

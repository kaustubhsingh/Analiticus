F.A.Q.


What is smajik?

Smajik is a webapp for finding most positive and most negative tweets about a topic


How does smajik work?

Smajik sources recent tweets having the queried keyword using Tweepy's API. Then Smajik runs the tweets through TextBlob Python library for sentiment analysis and sifts through the top positive and negative tweets
